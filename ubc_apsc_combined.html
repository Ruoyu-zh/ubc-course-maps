100 Level

📊 Course Flow

flowchart LR A[100 Intro I]-->B[101 Intro II] C[172 Analysis I]-->D[173 Analysis II] E[178 EM Waves] F[160 Computation] G[150 Case Studies] H[151 CAD Graphics]
APSC 100
Intro to Engineering I
工程导论I
3cr
Roles and responsibilities of the engineer, engineering disciplines.
工程师角色与职责、工程学科。
APSC 101
Intro to Engineering II
工程导论II
3cr
Engineering design process, sustainability, prototype testing.
工程设计过程、可持续性、原型测试。
Pre: APSC 100
APSC 150
Engineering Case Studies
工程案例研究
5cr
Application of scientific principles and technical knowledge to practical problems.
科学原理和技术知识在实践问题中的应用。
APSC 151
Computer-Aided Engineering Graphics
计算机辅助工程制图
3cr
Orthographic projection, technical sketching, 3D visualization.
正交投影、技术草图、三维可视化。
APSC 160
Intro to Computation in Engineering Design
工程设计计算导论
3cr
Analysis and simulation, data acquisition, measurement interfaces, engineering tools.
分析与仿真、数据采集、测量接口、工程工具。
APSC 172
Engineering Analysis I
工程分析I
3cr
Functions, limits, differentiation, applications of derivatives, integration.
函数、极限、微分、导数应用、积分。
APSC 173
Engineering Analysis II
工程分析II
3cr
Integrals, transcendental functions, techniques of integration, polar coordinates.
积分、超越函数、积分技巧、极坐标。
Pre: APSC 172
APSC 176
Engineering Communication
工程沟通
3cr
Purpose, audience, content, format, team-based report writings and presentations.
目的、受众、内容、格式、团队报告写作与演示。
Pre: LPI level 5
APSC 178
Electricity, Magnetism, and Waves
电磁学与波
4cr
Coulomb law, Gauss law, DC circuits, magnetic fields, EM induction, waves, optics.
库仑定律、高斯定律、直流电路、磁场、电磁感应、波、光学。
Pre: APSC 172
Co/Note: Co: APSC 173
APSC 179
Linear Algebra for Engineers
工程师线性代数
3cr
Systems of linear equations, Gaussian elimination, matrix operations, special matrices.
线性方程组、高斯消元、矩阵运算、特殊矩阵。
APSC 180
Statics
静力学
3cr
Force vectors, Cartesian coordinates, free body diagram, equilibrium of particles.
力向量、笛卡尔坐标系、自由体图、质点平衡。
APSC 182
Matter and Energy I
物质与能量I
3cr
Thermometry, states of matter, ideal/real gases, 1st/2nd laws of thermodynamics.
测温学、物质状态、理想/实际气体、热力学第一/第二定律。
APSC 183
Matter and Energy II
物质与能量II
3cr
Atomic structures and bonding, solid crystals, chemical equilibrium, reactions.
原子结构与键合、固体晶体、化学平衡、反应。

📊 Course Flow

flowchart LR A[201 Tech Comm]-->B[261 Tech Society] C[278 Materials]-->D[279 Materials Lab] B-->E[262 Society II]
APSC 201
Technical Communication
技术沟通
3cr
Written and oral communication in engineering. Report preparation, business correspondence.
工程书面与口头沟通。报告撰写、商务信函。
Pre: APSC 176/ENGL/WRDS 150
APSC 261
Technology and Society I
技术与社会I
3cr
Influence of technology on social, political, economic, and environmental aspects.
技术对社会、政治、经济和环境方面的影响。
APSC 262
Technology and Society II
技术与社会II
3cr
Continuation of technology-society themes.
技术-社会主题续篇。
APSC 278
Engineering Materials
工程材料
3cr
Atomic bonding, crystal structures, properties of metals/ceramics/polymers/wood/concrete.
原子键合、晶体结构、金属/陶瓷/聚合物/木材/混凝土性质。
APSC 279
Engineering Materials Laboratory
工程材料实验
1cr
Lab complement to APSC 278: material properties, corrosion, mechanical testing.
APSC 278配套实验：材料性质、腐蚀、力学测试。
Co/Note: Co: APSC 278

300+ Level

📊 Course Flow

flowchart LR A[364 Sustainability]-->B[366 Eng for Non-Eng] C[367 Humanitarian] D[383 Prototyping]-->E[486 New Venture] F[440 Management]-->G[450 Prof Practice] H[461 Leadership]
APSC 364
Applied Sustainability
应用可持续性
3cr
Role of common infrastructures, impact of technological solutions on people/economy/environment.
基础设施角色、技术方案对人/经济/环境的影响。
Pre: Third-year
APSC 366
The Art of the Possible: Engineering for Non-Engineers
工程入门(非工科)
3cr
Introduction to engineering for non-specialists via case studies from sustainability.
通过可持续性案例研究为非专业人士介绍工程。
APSC 367
Humanitarian Engineering
人道主义工程
3cr
Best practice, politics, ethics of technical solutions to humanitarian assistance.
人道主义援助技术方案的最佳实践、政治与伦理。
APSC 377
Nuclear Weapons and Arms Control
核武器与军备控制
3cr
Evolution and contemporary significance of nuclear weapons and arms control.
核武器与军备控制的演变与当代意义。
APSC 380
Introduction to Microcomputers
微机导论
3cr
Microcomputers in real-time or non-computational engineering applications.
实时或非计算工程应用中的微机。
APSC 383
Prototyping
原型设计
3cr
Systematic approach and use of tools for prototyping with software, electronics, physical components.
使用软件、电子、物理组件进行系统化原型设计。
APSC 402
Living Language: Science and Society
科学与社会的活语言
3cr
Multi-disciplinary: examine, integrate and apply subject-specific knowledge.
多学科：审视、整合和应用学科知识。
Pre: Fourth-year
APSC 440
Management Fundamentals for Tech Product Marketing
技术产品营销管理基础
3cr
Project management, marketing planning, product development, quality management.
项目管理、营销规划、产品开发、质量管理。
Pre: Fourth-year Eng
APSC 450
Professional Engineering Practice
专业工程实践
2cr
Legislation, ethical principles, management of engineering enterprises.
法规、伦理原则、工程企业管理。
Pre: Final year Eng
APSC 461
Global Engineering Leadership
全球工程领导力
3cr
Concepts, theory, and practice of engineering leadership.
工程领导力的概念、理论与实践。
Pre: Completed 3rd year
APSC 486
New Venture Design
新创企业设计
6cr
Teams create a business plan and a prototype or engineering solution.
团队创建商业计划和原型/工程方案。
APSC 496
Interdisciplinary Engineering Design Project
跨学科工程设计项目
1-9cr
Projects involving students from several departments.
涉及多个系学生的项目。
