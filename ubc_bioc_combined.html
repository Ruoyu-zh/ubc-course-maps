200 Level

📊 Course Flow

flowchart LR A[202 Medical Biochem]-->B[302 General Biochem] C[203 Fundamentals]-->D[303 Molecular Biochem] C-->B
BIOC 202
Introductory Medical Biochemistry
医学基础生化
3cr
Proteins, enzymes, carbohydrate metabolism, glucose homeostasis. Human biochemistry.
蛋白质、酶、碳水化合物代谢、葡萄糖稳态。人体生物化学。
Pre: CHEM 203/223/233
BIOC 203
Fundamentals of Biochemistry
生物化学基础
3cr
Amino acids, proteins, enzymology, human metabolic pathways, carbohydrate metabolism.
氨基酸、蛋白质、酶学、人体代谢途径、碳水化合物代谢。
Pre: BIOL 200 + CHEM 203/223
Co/Note: Co: CHEM 213

300 Level

📊 Course Flow

flowchart LR A[301 Lab]-->B[302/303 Core] C[304 Research]-->D[306 Quant Methods]
BIOC 301
Biochemistry Laboratory
生物化学实验
3cr
Techniques for studying chemical and physical properties of cellular components.
研究细胞组分化学和物理性质的技术。
Pre: CHEM 245/235 + 3rd year
Co/Note: Co: BIOC 302/303
BIOC 302
General Biochemistry
普通生物化学
3cr
Metabolism of lipids, steroids, amino acids, nucleotides; replication, transcription, translation.
脂类/类固醇/氨基酸/核苷酸代谢；复制、转录、翻译。
Pre: BIOL 201/BIOC 202/203 + CHEM
BIOC 303
Molecular Biochemistry
分子生物化学
6cr
Structure, function and metabolism of lipids, steroids, amino acids, nucleotides; gene regulation.
脂类/类固醇/氨基酸/核苷酸的结构/功能/代谢；基因调控。
Pre: BIOC 202/203/BIOL 201 + CHEM 213/233
BIOC 304
Contemporary Biochemical Research
当代生化研究
3cr
Biochemical research applied to medicine and biotechnology via experimental case studies.
通过实验案例研究应用于医学和生物技术的生化研究。
Pre: CHEM 213
Co/Note: Co: BIOC 303
BIOC 306
Quantitative Methods in Biochemistry
生化定量方法
3cr
Quantitative and computational methods: bioinformatics, computational biology, high-throughput data.
定量与计算方法：生物信息学、计算生物学、高通量数据。
Pre: BIOC 203(+303) or BIOC 202/BIOL 201(+302)

400 Level

📊 Course Flow

flowchart LR A[402 Proteins]-->B[403 Enzymology] A-->C[450 Membranes] D[410 Nucleic Acids]-->E[421 Recombinant DNA] D-->F[440 Molecular Bio]
BIOC 402
Proteins: Structure and Function
蛋白质：结构与功能
3cr
Protein structure, classification, purification, peptide synthesis, X-ray crystallography, NMR.
蛋白质结构、分类、纯化、肽合成、X射线晶体学、NMR。
Pre: BIOC 302/303/BIOT 380
BIOC 403
Enzymology
酶学
3cr
Kinetic analysis, catalytic mechanisms, transition state stabilization, regulation of activity.
动力学分析、催化机制、过渡态稳定、活性调控。
Pre: BIOC 402
BIOC 404
Biochemical Methods
生化方法
3cr
Theory and application of classical and emerging technologies in biochemical research.
经典与新兴技术在生化研究中的理论与应用。
Pre: Honours or instructor perm.
BIOC 410
Nucleic Acids: Structure and Function
核酸：结构与功能
3cr
Chemical, physical and biological properties of nucleic acids; gene expression regulation.
核酸的化学、物理和生物学性质；基因表达调控。
Pre: BIOC 303+BIOL 335 or BIOT 380
BIOC 420
Advanced Biochemical Techniques
高级生化技术
3cr
Multi-week experiments in contemporary biochemistry; experimental design, critical data analysis.
当代生物化学多周实验；实验设计、批判性数据分析。
Co/Note: Co: BIOC 404/FSCT 480
BIOC 421
Recombinant DNA Techniques
重组DNA技术
3cr
Contemporary nucleic acid research, cloning, recombinant protein engineering.
当代核酸研究、克隆、重组蛋白工程。
Pre: BIOC 301+410
BIOC 440
Concepts in Molecular Biology
分子生物学概念
3cr
Signal transduction, model eukaryotes, gene expression regulation.
信号转导、模式真核生物、基因表达调控。
Pre: BIOC 303/BIOT 380+402+410
BIOC 448
Directed Studies
指导研究
3-6cr
Library or lab project on specific topic agreed upon by faculty and student.
师生商定主题的图书馆或实验室项目。
Pre: Dept Head permission
BIOC 449
Honours Thesis
荣誉论文
3-6cr
Research problem under direction of faculty member.
教师指导下的研究问题。
BIOC 450
Membrane Biochemistry
膜生物化学
3cr
Biochemistry of membranes, membrane proteins, membrane fusion, electrical signaling.
膜与膜蛋白生物化学、膜融合、电信号。
Pre: BIOC 303/BIOT 380+402+410
BIOC 470
Biochemistry & Society
生化与社会
3cr
Biochemical basis of global challenges; critical evaluation of primary literature.
全球挑战的生化基础；原始文献批判性评估。
Pre: BIOC 302/303
