Mining Engineering Program

Bachelor of Applied Science (BASc) · Norman B. Keevil Institute of Mining Engineering

📋 项目概览 Program Overview

采矿工程关注矿产资源的最优开采，同时最小化环境影响。该学科需要广泛的工程和科学知识。课程涵盖采矿方法、岩石力学、矿物加工、矿山规划和矿山经济。

Mining Engineering concerns the optimal exploitation of mineral resources while minimizing environmental impact. Requires broad knowledge of engineering and scientific subjects.
