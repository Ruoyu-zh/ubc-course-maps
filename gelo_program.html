Geological Engineering Program

Bachelor of Applied Science (BASc) · Interdisciplinary (EOAS + Civil + Mining)

📋 项目概览 Program Overview

地质工程是应用科学院下的跨学科专业，由地球、海洋与大气科学系（EOAS）管理。项目结合地质科学、土木工程和采矿工程的核心知识，培养能够解决岩土工程、地质灾害、资源开发和地质环境问题的工程师。项目包含户外野外学校（Field School）实践环节。

Geological Engineering is an interdisciplinary program administered by EOAS, combining geological sciences, civil engineering, and mining engineering. Includes field school components. Contact: Dr. Scott McDougall, Director (smcdouga@eoas.ubc.ca).
