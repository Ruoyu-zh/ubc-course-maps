BIOL 111
Introduction to Modern Biology
现代生物学导论
3 学分
Genetic foundation of variation, evolution, infectious disease, cancer causes, population growth, human impacts on ecosystems. NOT open to students with Biology 12 credit.
前置: None
BIOL 112
Biology of the Cell
细胞生物学
3 学分
Cellular and molecular biology: macromolecules, membrane structure, organelles, gene expression, cell division. Required for most BIOL specializations.
前置: BIOL 111 + CHEM 12/100/111
BIOL 121
Genetics, Evolution and Ecology
遗传、进化与生态学
3 学分
Mendelian genetics, population genetics, natural selection, speciation, ecological interactions. Gateway to genetics and ecology streams.
前置: BIOL 111 + (BIOL 11 or 12 or BIOL 111)
BIOL 140
Laboratory Investigation in Biology
生物学实验探究
2 学分
Hands-on lab skills: microscopy, experimental design, data collection, scientific writing for biology.
前置: BIOL 111
BIOL 180
Think Like a Biologist
像生物学家一样思考
2 学分
Scientific reasoning, experimental design, data interpretation in biology. Focus on thinking skills over memorization.
前置: BIOL 111

BIOL 200
Fundamentals of Cell Biology
细胞生物学基础
3 学分
Membrane models, organelles, gene-to-protein, endomembrane system, secretion, endocytosis, transport, cytoskeleton, cell motility. Gateway to ALL upper-level BIOL.
前置: BIOL 112 + CHEM 123/130
BIOL 201
Introduction to Biochemistry
生物化学导论
3 学分
Protein structure/enzyme kinetics, metabolic pathways, bioenergetics. Credit exclusion with BIOC 202/203.
前置: BIOL 200 + CHEM 233/260
BIOL 234
Fundamentals of Genetics
遗传学基础
3 学分
Chromosomal basis of inheritance, linkage, molecular genetics, gene regulation, population genetics.
前置: BIOL 121 + BIOL 200
BIOL 260
Fundamentals of Physiology
生理学基础
3 学分
Animal physiology: nervous system, muscle, cardiovascular, respiratory, renal, endocrine systems.
前置: BIOL 200

BIOL 300
Fundamentals of Biostatistics
生物统计学基础
3 学分
Statistical methods for biological data: hypothesis testing, ANOVA, regression, experimental design. R-based analysis.
前置: BIOL 200 + STAT 200/201/BIOL 300-level
BIOL 301
Biomathematics
生物数学
3 学分
Mathematical modeling of biological systems: differential equations, dynamical systems, ecological and evolutionary models.
前置: MATH 100-level + BIOL 200
BIOL 335
Molecular Genetics
分子遗传学
3 学分
Gene structure, DNA replication, transcription, translation, gene regulation, genomics, recombinant DNA technology.
前置: BIOL 234
BIOL 336
Evolutionary Genetics
进化遗传学
3 学分
Population genetics theory, molecular evolution, phylogenetics, genome evolution.
前置: BIOL 234
BIOL 371
Principles of Neurobiology I
神经生物学原理 I
3 学分
Cellular and molecular neuroscience: neurons, synapses, neurotransmitters, action potentials, sensory transduction.
前置: BIOL 200 + (BIOL 260 或 NSCI 200)
BIOL 372
Principles of Neurobiology II
神经生物学原理 II
3 学分
Systems neuroscience: neural circuits, plasticity, learning & memory, motor systems, sensory processing.
前置: BIOL 371

BIOL 425
Computational Genomics
计算基因组学
3 学分
Bioinformatics approaches to genome analysis: sequence alignment, assembly, annotation, comparative genomics, RNA-seq.
前置: BIOL 335 or CPSC 301
BIOL 426
Genome Evolution
基因组进化
3 学分
How genomes evolve: gene duplication, transposable elements, horizontal transfer, genome size evolution.
前置: BIOL 336
BIOL 428
Epigenetics
表观遗传学
3 学分
Mechanisms of gene regulation beyond DNA sequence: chromatin modification, DNA methylation, non-coding RNAs.
前置: BIOL 335
BIOL 451
Comparative Neurobiology
比较神经生物学
3 学分
Evolution of nervous systems across species. Comparing neural circuits and behaviors in different organisms.
前置: BIOL 371 or BIOL 372 or PSYC 304/370
BIOL 458
Developmental Neurobiology
发育神经生物学
3 学分
How the nervous system develops: neural induction, axon guidance, synapse formation, neurotrophic factors.
前置: None listed (check calendar)
BIOL 459
Neurobiology of Sensory and Motor Systems
感觉与运动神经生物学
3 学分
How sensory information is encoded and how motor commands are generated. Visual, auditory, somatosensory systems.
前置: BIOL 372 or PSYC 304/370
BIOL 460
Neurobiology of Vision
视觉神经生物学
3 学分
Visual system from retina to cortex. Phototransduction, receptive fields, color vision, motion processing.
前置: BIOL 372 or PSYC 304/370
BIOL 463
Gene Regulation in Development
发育基因调控
3 学分
Transcriptional control of embryonic development. HOX genes, signaling pathways, stem cell regulation.
前置: BIOL 335
BIOL 464
Animal Developmental Genetics
动物发育遗传学
3 学分
Genetic analysis of development in model organisms. Drosophila, C. elegans, zebrafish, mouse.
前置: BIOL 335
BIOL 465
Diversity of Biological Lab Research
生物学实验研究实践
3 学分
Laboratory research experience in a biology lab. Independent project work under faculty supervision.
前置: Instructor permission

Graduate-Level BIOL Courses (500+)

BIOL 500+ courses are for graduate students. Qualified undergraduates may enroll with instructor permission. Key courses: BIOL 501 (Evolutionary Biology), 503 (Ecology), 509 (Population Genetics), 525 (Computational Genomics), 530 (Cell Biology), 533 (Developmental Biology), 535 (Genetics), 537 (Plant Biology), 539 (Conservation Biology), 548 (Advanced Topics), 560 (Neurobiology).
