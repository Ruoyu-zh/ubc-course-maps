BMEG 310
Introduction to Bioinformatics
生物信息学导论
4 学分
Bioinformatics methods, databases and tools for genomics, proteomics, structural biology, and evolutionary biology.
前置: BMEG 245, CPEN 221/223
BMEG 320
Bioengineering Feedback Systems and Controls
生物工程反馈系统与控制
3 学分
Dynamic system modeling, state space, linearization, feedback, stability, control design with biological examples.
前置: BMEG 220 或 (ELEC 221+201+202)
BMEG 321
Biomedical Instrumentation
生物医学仪器
3 学分
Medical instruments for nervous, respiratory, circulatory systems; biopotential signals, medical imaging, robotic surgery.
前置: MATH 256 + (BMEG 220 或 (ELEC 211+ELEC 201))
BMEG 330
Biomechanics II
生物力学 II
3 学分
Sports, orthopaedic, injury, and cellular biomechanics. Performance improvement, implant design, cancer cell identification.
前置: BMEG 230
BMEG 350
Human Structure/Function from Cells to Systems
人体结构与功能
4 学分
Human anatomy, histology, and physiology. Anatomical compartmentation, homeostatic control, intercellular communication.
前置: BMEG 245, BMEG 250
BMEG 357
Biomedical Engineering Design II
生物医学工程设计 II
3 学分
Creating biomedical technology innovations through supervised design for clinical clients. Prototype building and testing.
前置: BMEG 257
BMEG 371
Transport Phenomena within Cells and Tissues
细胞与组织输运现象
3 学分
Fluid flow and mass transport in healthy/diseased cells/tissues. Drug delivery, tissue engineering applications.
前置: MATH 253, MATH 256
BMEG 372
Biomedical Materials and Drug Delivery
生物医学材料与药物递送
3 学分
Biomaterials science for drug delivery. Biocompatibility, degradation, nanocarriers, liposomes, nucleic acid delivery.
前置: BMEG 250, CHEM 123
BMEG 373
Microfluidics
微流控
3 学分
Miniaturizing complex fluid flow processes. Liquid flow manipulation, electrophoresis, integrated platforms.
前置: 无
BMEG 374
Cellular Bioengineering Laboratory
细胞生物工程实验
3 学分
Fundamental genetic, molecular, and tissue engineering techniques. Design of experiments for cellular bioengineering.
前置: BMEG 245

合成生物学强化

可穿戴医疗技术

前置: BMEG 310, BMEG 321
BMEG 429

Microfluidic fabrication, flow control, analysis. Hands-on lab in biomedicine, materials, and chemistry.

Clinical and Industrial Biomedical Engineering
临床与工业生物医学工程
3 学分
Clinical practice, Canadian healthcare, medical diagnosis, ethics and regulations for clinical trials.
前置: 无

生物医学工程设计项目

Capstone design of a practical biomedical device or system. Projects from local industry and research labs.
前置: BMEG 350, BMEG 357
BMEG 470
Cellular Responses to Forces and Biomaterials
细胞对力与生物材料的响应
3 学分
Mechanical forces and biomaterials regulating cellular functions in tissue development, regeneration and repair.
前置: BMEG 245, BMEG 250, BMEG 350
BMEG 474
Stem Cells and Regenerative Medicine
干细胞与再生医学

Stem cell biology and applications in regenerative medicine. Disease modeling, regulatory/commercialization.
