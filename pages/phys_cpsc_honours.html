Combined Honours in Physics & Computer Science

BSc Combined Honours · Department of Physics & Astronomy + Department of Computer Science

Program Overview

This program is designed for strong students with an interest in both Physics and Computer Science. Graduates are well prepared for graduate studies in either Department or can go directly into the workforce. Graduates who focus on Computational Physics are highly sought after by major North American centres for graduate research in Computational Science. A Co-op option is available.

适合对物理和计算机科学都有浓厚兴趣的学生。毕业生可选择进入研究生院（物理或CS方向）或直接就业。专注于计算物理的毕业生在北美计算科学研究中心非常受欢迎。可选 Co-op。
