📋 项目概览 Program Overview

计算机工程专业从大二开始（完成大一工程基础课程后）。学生将获得计算机工程应用科学学士学位。课程涵盖硬件设计、嵌入式系统、软件开发和计算机体系结构。学生可选择专注于计算机硬件设计或软件密集型计算系统方向← br>
The Computer Engineering Program begins in second year after completing the required first-year engineering courses. Students graduate with a BASc in Computer Engineering, with elective options to focus on hardware design or software-intensive systems.

🔑 核心课程简← Core Course Highlights

CPEN 211 · Introduction to Microcomputers (5 cr)
学生将在 FPGA 开发板上实现自己的微处理器，能够运← ARM 汇编语言子集。涵盖数字逻辑、计算机体系结构和硬← 软件接口的基础知识← /div>
By the end of the course, students typically implement their own microprocessor (on an FPGA board) that can run a subset of ARM assembly language.
CPEN 221 · Introduction to Software Construction (5 cr)
多个小型项目，涉及音频文件处理、社交网络大规模图分析、含AI元素的游戏开发。同时学习编译器开发的关键概念← /div>
Mini-projects may involve processing audio files, working with large graphs that represent social networks, or a game that utilizes some AI. Also covers key ideas for developing compilers.
CPEN 291 / 391 · Computer Systems Design Studio (6 cr)
硬件与软件综合设计项目，通常涉及机器人技术和自主小车控制← /div>
Projects involve hardware and software design, often relating to robotics and controlling a small autonomous vehicle.
CPEN 491 · Capstone Design Project (10 cr)
为期一年的毕业设计项目，综合运用所学知识完成完整的计算机工程设计← /div>
Year-long comprehensive design project integrating all aspects of computer engineering.
