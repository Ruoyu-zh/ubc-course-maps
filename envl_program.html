The ENVL program combines chemical, biological, and civil engineering fundamentals with an ecosystem approach to environmental problems. Students apply during Term 2 of first-year engineering. Graduates are uniquely qualified for environmental facility design, impact assessment, sustainability planning, and policy formulation. Note: this is different from the Joint UNBC/UBC Environmental Engineering program.
