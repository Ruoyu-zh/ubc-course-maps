📋 项目概览 Program Overview

电气工程专业从大二开始（完成大一工程基础课程后）。学生将获得电气工程应用科学学士学位。课程涵盖电路设计、信号处理、电磁学、电力系统、控制系统和通信系统。

The Electrical Engineering Program begins in second year after completing the required first-year engineering courses. Students graduate with a BASc in Electrical Engineering. The curriculum spans circuit design, signal processing, electromagnetics, power systems, control systems, and communications.

🔑 核心课程简介 Core Course Highlights

ELEC 201 / 202 · Circuit Analysis (4+4 cr)
大二核心课程，涵盖直流和交流电路分析、运算放大器、瞬态响应和频率响应。为所有后续 EE 课程奠定基础。
Core second-year sequence covering DC/AC circuit analysis, op-amps, transient and frequency response. Foundation for all subsequent EE coursework.
ELEC 221 · Signals and Systems (4 cr)
信号处理入门：傅里叶分析、拉普拉斯变换、采样定理、线性时不变系统。
Fourier analysis, Laplace transforms, sampling theorem, LTI systems. Mathematical foundation for EE and CPEN.
ELEC 291 / 391 · Design Studio (6+6 cr)
电气工程设计实践，从概念到原型，涵盖 PCB 设计、焊接、测试和项目管理。
Hands-on design studio from concept to prototype: PCB design, soldering, testing, and project management.
ELEC 491 · Capstone Design Project (10 cr)
为期一年的毕业设计项目，综合运用电气工程知识完成完整的系统设计。
Year-long comprehensive design project integrating all aspects of electrical engineering.

🧬 生物医学工程方向 Biomedical Engineering Option

生物医学工程方向是电气工程专业下的一个专门方向。学生在 ELEC 课程基础上，集中于医学和生命科学的技术应用。完成该方向的学生将获得 Bachelor of Applied Science in Electrical Engineering (Biomedical Engineering Option)。

需在大一结束后向方向协调员申请，基于对生物医学工程的兴趣和一年级 GPA。被录取后将修读修改版的电气工程标准课程。

The Biomedical Engineering Option allows ELEC students to concentrate coursework and projects on the application of technology to medicine and the life sciences. Admission at end of first year by permission of the option coordinator.

🔑 BMEG 核心课程简介 Core Highlights

BMEG 410 · Biomedical Instrumentation (4 cr)
生物医学仪器原理与设计，涵盖传感器、信号采集、放大和滤波电路，以及医疗设备的安全标准。
Principles and design of biomedical instruments: sensors, signal acquisition, amplification, filtering, and medical device safety standards.
BMEG 456 · Biosignals and Systems (3 cr)
生物信号（ECG, EEG, EMG）的采集、处理和分析，以及生理系统建模。
Acquisition, processing, and analysis of biosignals (ECG, EEG, EMG) and physiological system modeling.
ELEC 471 · Medical Imaging (3 cr)
医学成像技术：X射线、CT、MRI、超声、PET 的物理原理和图像重建方法。
Medical imaging modalities: X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound, PET — physical principles and image reconstruction.
EECE 491 · Biomedical Capstone (10 cr)
为期一年的生物医学工程毕业设计，在医疗技术领域完成完整的工程设计项目。
Year-long biomedical engineering capstone: complete design project in medical technology.
