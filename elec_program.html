📋 项目概览 Program Overview

电气工程专业从大二开始（完成大一工程基础课程后）。学生将获得电气工程应用科学学士学位。课程涵盖电路设计、信号处理、电磁学、电力系统、控制系统和通信系统。电气工程是涵盖最广泛的工程学科之一，从纳米电子学到电力传输网络。

The Electrical Engineering Program begins in second year after completing the required first-year engineering courses. Students graduate with a BASc in Electrical Engineering. The curriculum spans circuit design, signal processing, electromagnetics, power systems, control systems, and communications — one of the broadest engineering disciplines.

📌 选修课详情 Elective Details

⚡ Breadth & Advanced Electives (22 cr)

Breadth Electives: Chosen to ensure broad coverage across EE sub-disciplines. Refer to program checklist.

Advanced Electives: 400-level ELEC courses (and select CPEN 400-level):

  • ELEC 421 / ELEC 422 — (only one may count)
  • ELEC 499 — max 3 credits toward Advanced Electives
  • ELEC 400M, CPEN 400D — approved Advanced Electives
  • Only some CPEN 400-level courses qualify

See ELEC Advanced Elective List (Notion)

📖 Complementary Studies (9 cr)

Humanities & Social Studies (6 cr): Courses in arts, humanities, social sciences, or business. Max 3 credits in language courses. Typically 3 credits completed in first year.

Impact of Technology on Society (3 cr): Courses examining the relationship between technology and society.

See: EAS Course Planning Page

🔬 Science Elective (3 cr)

3 credits from an approved science department. Designed to provide an introduction to the practice of the scientific method. See the Science Elective Checklist.

🆓 Free Electives (3 cr)

3 credits of free electives. Can extend beyond engineering or science. May be applied toward ECE requirements and Minor requirements (e.g., Mathematics Minor, Commerce Minor).

🔑 核心课程简介 Core Course Highlights

ELEC 201 / 202 · Circuit Analysis (4+4 cr)
大二核心课程，涵盖直流和交流电路分析、运算放大器、瞬态响应和频率响应。为所有后续 EE 课程奠定基础。
Core second-year sequence covering DC/AC circuit analysis, op-amps, transient and frequency response. Foundation for all subsequent EE coursework.
ELEC 221 · Signals and Systems (4 cr)
信号处理入门：傅里叶分析、拉普拉斯变换、采样定理、线性时不变系统。EE 和 CPEN 的数学基础课程。
Introduction to signal processing: Fourier analysis, Laplace transforms, sampling theorem, LTI systems. Mathematical foundation for EE and CPEN.
ELEC 291 / 391 · Design Studio (6+6 cr)
电气工程设计实践课程，从概念到原型，涵盖 PCB 设计、焊接、测试和项目管理。大二和大三各一次。
Hands-on design studio from concept to prototype: PCB design, soldering, testing, and project management. Offered in both second and third year.
ELEC 491 · Capstone Design Project (10 cr)
为期一年的毕业设计项目，综合运用电气工程知识完成完整的系统设计。
Year-long comprehensive design project integrating all aspects of electrical engineering.
