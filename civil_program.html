📋 项目概览 Program Overview

土木工程专业从大二开始（完成大一工程基础年后）。课程涵盖结构工程、岩土工程、水利工程、交通工程和环境工程。大四可选择多个技术方向（结构、岩土、水利、交通、环境等）。Prospective students should complete WRDS 150 before entry to avoid an additional year.

The Civil Engineering program begins in second year after completing first year engineering. The curriculum covers structural, geotechnical, hydraulic, transportation, and environmental engineering. Multiple technical elective streams available in fourth year.
