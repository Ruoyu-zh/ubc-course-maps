📘 100/200级

10 courses

ELEC 201
Circuit Analysis I
电路分析I
4 学分
Fundamentals of lumped linear time-invariant circuits; network theorems; op-amps; first order circuits; DC analysis of diodes, BJT, FET.
集总线性时不变电路基础；网络定理；运算放大器；一阶电路；二极管/BJT/FET的直流分析。
前置: MATH 101 + PHYS 108/118/158
并修/注意: 并修: MATH 255/256
ELEC 202
Circuit Analysis II
电路分析II
4 学分
Phasor analysis, AC three phase power; transfer functions; Bode plots; filters; resonance; Laplace transforms; transformers; two-port networks.
相量分析、交流三相功率；传递函数；Bode图；滤波器；谐振；拉普拉斯变换；变压器；二端口网络。
前置: MATH 101 + PHYS 118/158 + MATH 255/256 + ELEC 201/203
ELEC 203
Basic Circuit Analysis
基础电路分析
3 学分
Ideal passive elements and sources; Kirchhoff's Laws; DC circuits; first order circuits; op-amps; impedance; phasors; complex power. Not open to ECE students.
理想无源元件和源；基尔霍夫定律；直流电路；一阶电路；运放；阻抗；相量；复功率。非ECE学生。
前置: MATH 101 + PHYS 108/118/158
并修/注意: 并修: MATH 255/256
ELEC 204
Linear Circuits
线性电路
4 学分
Basic concepts and analysis techniques for electric and electronic circuits including Bode plots and Laplace transform. RLC circuits, phasors, op-amps, nonlinear elements.
电路与电子电路基本概念与分析技术：Bode图、拉普拉斯变换、RLC电路、相量、运放、非线性元件。
前置: MATH 152 + PHYS 108/118/158
并修/注意: 并修: MATH 255/256
ELEC 205
Electronics Laboratory
电子学实验
1 学分
Experiments involving electronic devices and circuits.
电子器件与电路实验。
并修/注意: 并修: ELEC 204
ELEC 211
Engineering Electromagnetics
工程电磁学
2 学分
Electrostatics, electric currents, dielectrics, capacitance, electrostatic potential, magnetostatics.
静电场、电流、电介质、电容、静电势、静磁场。
前置: MATH 253 + APSC 178/PHYS 108/118/158
并修/注意: 并修: MATH 264
ELEC 221
Signals and Systems
信号与系统
4 学分
Complex numbers, LTI systems, convolution, discrete-time Fourier series/transforms, z-transform, sampling, filtering, modulation, feedback, stability.
复数、LTI系统、卷积、离散时间傅里叶级数/变换、z变换、采样、滤波、调制、反馈、稳定性。
前置: MATH 255/256
ELEC 281
Technical Communication
技术沟通
3 学分
Written and oral communication in engineering. Technical description, report preparation, business correspondence, oral presentation.
工程中的书面和口头沟通：技术描述、报告撰写、商务信函、口头报告。
前置: APSC 176/ENGL 110/111/112/120/121/WRDS 150
ELEC 291
EE Design Studio I
电子工程设计工作室I
6 学分
Design projects involving electronic devices and circuits, electromagnetics, signals/systems, microcomputers.
涉及电子器件电路、电磁学、信号系统、微机的设计项目。
前置: ELEC 201 + CPEN 211
ELEC 292
BME Design Studio
生物医学工程设计工作室
6 学分
Design projects involving electronic devices/circuits, electromagnetics, signals/systems, microcomputers with biomedical applications.
涉及电子器件电路、电磁学、信号系统、微机及生物医学应用的设计项目。
并修/注意: 并修: ELEC 201 + CPEN 211 + ELEC 221

📙 300级

14 courses

ELEC 301
Electronic Circuits
电子电路
4 学分
Analysis and design of electronic circuits; biasing, small-signal models, frequency response of amplifiers, feedback, stability, op-amps, active filters, oscillators.
电子电路分析与设计：偏置、小信号模型、放大器频率响应、反馈、稳定性、运放、有源滤波器、振荡器。
前置: ELEC 201/203 + ELEC 202 或 ELEC 204
ELEC 302
Electronic Circuits for Electromechanical Design
机电设计电子电路
3 学分
Semiconductor fundamentals; modelling of diodes and transistors; power supplies; waveform generators; logic circuits; op-amps; filters; oscillators.
半导体基础；二极管和晶体管建模；电源设计；波形发生器；逻辑电路；运放；滤波器；振荡器。
前置: ELEC 201/203/204 或 MECH 221
ELEC 311
Electromagnetic Fields and Waves
电磁场与波
4 学分
Waves and propagation; Maxwell's equations; transmission lines; impedance matching/Smith charts; reflection/refraction; waveguides; antennas.
波与传播；麦克斯韦方程组；传输线；阻抗匹配/Smith圆图；反射/折射；波导；天线。
前置: ELEC 211 + MATH 253 + MATH 264 + MATH 255/256
ELEC 315
Electronic Materials and Devices
电子材料与器件
4 学分
Fundamental aspects of semiconductors, metals, dielectrics, piezoelectrics, magnetic materials and device applications.
半导体、金属、电介质、压电材料、磁性材料基础及器件应用。
ELEC 321
Stochastic Signals and Systems
随机信号与系统
4 学分
Stochastic behaviour of signals and systems; discrete/continuous probability; random processes; LTI system modelling; binary hypothesis testing.
信号与系统的随机行为；离散/连续概率；随机过程；LTI系统建模；二元假设检验。
前置: ELEC 221 或 STAT 305
ELEC 331
Computer Communications
计算机通信
4 学分
Analysis, design and implementation of computer networks and protocols. Application layer, TCP, IP, routing, reliable data transfer, multiple access, Ethernet.
计算机网络与协议的分析、设计与实现：应用层、TCP、IP、路由、可靠数据传输、多址接入、以太网。
前置: ELEC 221/CPEN 212/CPEN 331
ELEC 341
Systems and Control
系统与控制
4 学分
Continuous time system analysis by Laplace transforms; transfer function/state space modelling; feedback, stability, sensitivity; control design; frequency domain.
连续时间系统拉普拉斯分析；传递函数/状态空间建模；反馈、稳定性、灵敏度；控制设计；频域分析。
前置: ELEC 202 或 ELEC 204
ELEC 342
Electro-Mechanical Energy Conversion
机电能量转换
4 学分
Three phase power; transformers; magnetic circuits; electromechanical energy conversion; DC machines; AC machines; variable frequency operation.
三相电力；变压器；磁路；机电能量转换；直流电机；交流电机；变频运行。
前置: PHYS 170 + ELEC 202
ELEC 343
Electromechanics
机电学
3 学分
Electromechanical energy conversion; linear actuators; torque/speed; reluctance, stepper, PM, induction, brushless-DC motors; drive circuits.
机电能量转换；线性执行器；转矩/速度；磁阻/步进/永磁/感应/无刷直流电机；驱动电路。
前置: ELEC 202 或 ELEC 302
ELEC 344
Applied Electronics and Electromechanics
应用电子与机电学
3 学分
Semiconductor devices; analog circuits; force/torque production; motor principles; power electronics. Not open to ECE.
半导体器件；模拟电路；力/转矩产生；电机原理；电力电子。非ECE学生。
前置: ELEC 201/203/204 或 MECH 221
ELEC 352
Electric Energy Systems
电能系统
4 学分
Three-phase energy systems; high-voltage transmission; low-voltage distribution; renewable electricity generation; grid integration.
三相能源系统；高压输电；低压配电；可再生发电；电网整合。
前置: ELEC 202 + ELEC 342
ELEC 361
Molecules to Mechanisms
从分子到机械
4 学分
Forces, scaling, thermal, fluidic, and mechanical properties for design of emerging devices/systems at micro/nano scales.
微纳尺度新兴器件与系统设计中的力、缩放、热、流体和机械特性。
ELEC 371
Biomedical Engineering Instrumentation
生物医学工程仪器
3 学分
Theory and experiments involving instruments for surgery and sensors for physiological measurements.
涉及外科手术仪器和生理测量传感器的理论与实验。
前置: MATH 255/256 + (ELEC 202+292 或 BMEG 220+257)
ELEC 391
EE Design Studio II
电子工程设计工作室II
6 学分
Introduction to project management; problem definition; design principles; circuit/software design; solid modelling; PCBs; assembly; testing; presentations.
项目管理导论；问题定义；设计原理；电路/软件设计；实体建模；PCB；装配；测试；演示。
前置: CPEN 291/ELEC 291/292 + 两门 ELEC 301/341/311/342

📕 400级

33 courses

ELEC 401
Analog CMOS Integrated Circuit Design
模拟CMOS集成电路设计
4 学分
Design and analysis of analog ICs: gain stages, op-amp design, frequency compensation, oscillators, A/D, D/A, PLLs, DLLs, RF intro.
模拟IC设计与分析：增益级、运放设计、频率补偿、振荡器、A/D、D/A、PLL、DLL、RF简介。
前置: ELEC 202 或 ELEC 204
ELEC 402
Introduction to VLSI Systems
VLSI系统导论
4 学分
Static CMOS combinational logic; standard cell design/layout; memory element design; dynamic logic; synchronous logic; timing; power; scaling; VHDL/Verilog CAD project.
静态CMOS组合逻辑；标准单元设计/版图；存储元件设计；动态逻辑；同步逻辑；时序；功耗；缩放；VHDL/Verilog CAD项目。
前置: CPEN 311
ELEC 403
Advanced Digital VLSI Systems
高级数字VLSI系统
3 学分
Logical effort; noise; low power techniques; power distribution; SRAM/DRAM design; non-volatile memory; high-speed arithmetic; clock trees; synchronization.
逻辑努力；噪声；低功耗技术；电源分配；SRAM/DRAM设计；非易失存储；高速算术；时钟树；同步。
前置: ELEC 402
ELEC 404
RF Integrated Circuits
射频集成电路
3 学分
RF communication systems; matching networks; transceiver architectures; low noise amplifiers; mixers; oscillators.
射频通信系统；匹配网络；收发器架构；低噪声放大器；混频器；振荡器。
前置: ELEC 221
并修/注意: 并修: ELEC 401
ELEC 411
Antennas and Propagation
天线与传播
3 学分
Basic antenna concepts; antennas for various frequencies; terrestrial/satellite propagation; environmental effects on EM radiation.
基本天线概念；各频段天线；地面/卫星传播；环境对电磁辐射的影响。
前置: ELEC 311/PHYS 301/PHYS 354
ELEC 412
Optical Waveguides and Photonics
光波导与光子学
3 学分
Planar dielectric waveguides; single mode optical fibers; integrated optics; resonators; transfer matrix methods; optical communications links.
平面介质波导；单模光纤；集成光学；谐振器；传输矩阵法；光通信链路。
ELEC 413
Semiconductor Lasers
半导体激光器
3 学分
Introduction to lasers and applications; theory, modelling, fabrication and performance of semiconductor lasers; optical communications links.
激光器与应用导论；半导体激光器理论、建模、制造和性能；光通信链路。
ELEC 415
Semiconductor Devices: Physics, Design and Analysis
半导体器件：物理、设计与分析
3 学分
Physics, design and analysis of semiconductor devices: solar cells, LEDs, high-speed MOSFETs, high-frequency HBTs, low-noise HEMTs.
半导体器件物理、设计与分析：太阳能电池、LED、高速MOSFET、高频HBT、低噪声HEMT。
前置: ELEC 315 或 PHYS 304
ELEC 421
Digital Signal and Image Processing
数字信号与图像处理
4 学分
Digital signal processing fundamentals; image processing; signal representation; filtering; statistical estimation; biomedical applications.
数字信号处理基础；图像处理；信号表示；滤波；统计估计；生物医学应用。
前置: ELEC 221 + CPEN 221/223/CPSC 259
ELEC 422
Biosignals and Systems
生物信号与系统
3 学分
Data acquisition; time/frequency domain analysis; analog/discrete filter design; sampling; time-dependent processing; linear prediction; random signals; biomedical modelling.
数据采集；时频域分析；模拟/离散滤波器设计；采样；时变处理；线性预测；随机信号；生物医学建模。
前置: ELEC 371+341+221 + CPSC 259/260 + STAT 251/MATH 302/318/STAT 302
ELEC 431
Communication Systems I
通信系统I
4 学分
Review of probability, signals/noise; digital communication over Gaussian noise channels; optimal receiver design; modulation; error performance; info/coding theory; wireless.
概率论复习、信号/噪声；高斯噪声信道数字通信；最优接收机设计；调制；误码性能；信息/编码论；无线通信。
前置: ELEC 221 + MATH 302/318/STAT 251/302
ELEC 432
Communication Systems II
通信系统II
3 学分
Communication over bandlimited AWGN channels; power spectra; intersymbol interference; coding schemes; spread spectrum; security; wireless standards.
带限AWGN信道通信；功率谱；码间干扰；编码方案；扩频；安全；无线标准。
前置: ELEC 431
ELEC 441
Control Systems
控制系统
3 学分
Linear control systems: system parameters vs responses; design specifications for dynamic/steady-state performance; robust PID; multivariable controller design.
线性控制系统：系统参数与响应关系；动态/稳态性能设计规格；鲁棒PID；多变量控制器设计。
前置: ELEC 341
ELEC 442
Introduction to Robotics
机器人学导论
4 学分
Rigid motions; homogeneous transformations; forward/inverse kinematics; velocity kinematics; motion planning; trajectory generation; sensing; vision; control.
刚体运动；齐次变换；正/逆运动学；速度运动学；运动规划；轨迹生成；传感；视觉；控制。
前置: PHYS 170 + MATH 152/221
ELEC 451
Power Electronics
电力电子
4 学分
Power diodes, thyristors, transistors, FETs; AC-DC, DC-DC, DC-AC, AC-AC converters; modulation techniques; gating circuits; practical design.
功率二极管、晶闸管、晶体管、FET；AC-DC/DC-DC/DC-AC/AC-AC变换器；调制技术；门极电路；实践设计。
前置: ELEC 301/344/342
ELEC 452
Industrial Drives
工业驱动
3 学分
Mechanical loads; dynamic/static characteristics of DC and AC drives; microprocessor-based controllers; commercial drive selection.
机械负载；交直流驱动动态/静态特性；微处理器控制器；商用驱动选型。
前置: ELEC 342 或 ELEC 343
ELEC 453
Power System Analysis
电力系统分析
4 学分
Transmission/distribution; phasors; complex power; balanced/unbalanced three-phase; symmetrical components; sequence networks; voltage regulation; short circuit capacity.
输配电；相量；复功率；平衡/不平衡三相；对称分量；序网络；电压调整；短路容量。
前置: ELEC 342
ELEC 454
Power System Analysis II
电力系统分析II
4 学分
Power system monitoring/control; large networks; automatic generation control; optimum power flow; traveling wave transmission lines; EMTP/MATLAB for transients/stability.
电力系统监控；大网络；自动发电控制；最优潮流；行波传输线；EMTP/MATLAB暂态/稳定分析。
前置: ELEC 453
ELEC 455
Power Systems Protection
电力系统保护
3 学分
Analysis of disturbances; security; cascading/blackouts; protection role/impact; transducers; measuring elements; transmission/distribution protection.
扰动分析；安全；级联/停电；保护作用与影响；传感器；测量元件；输配电保护。

电子学中的纳米技术

微传感器、执行器和MEMS的机理、设计、制造和测试。
