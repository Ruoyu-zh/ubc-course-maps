📋 项目概览 Program Overview

UBC 化学与生物工程系提供两个方向的 BASc 学位：Chemical Engineering（化学工程）和 Chemical & Biological Engineering（化学与生物工程）。学生完成大一工程基础年后进入大二。两个方向在前两年课程基本相同，大三和大四根据所选方向有差异。

The CHBE department offers two BASc streams: Chemical Engineering and Chemical & Biological Engineering. Both share a common second year, then diverge in years 3 and 4 with stream-specific courses and capstone projects.

⚗️ 两个方向的关键区别 Key Differences

Chemical Engineering 化学工程

Year 3 特有课程：APSC 278 (材料工程), CHBE 366 (化学工程实验), APSC 262 (技术与社会)

Year 4 Capstone：CHBE 454 — Chemical Process and Product Design (8 cr)

Year 4 核心课：CHBE 471 — Chemical Process Engineering (4 cr)

侧重传统化学工程：催化、反应器设计、化工流程与产品设计。

Chemical & Biological Engineering 化学与生物工程

Year 3 特有课程：APSC 262 (技术与社会), CHBE 365 (生物工程实验), CHBE 381 (生物过程工程 I)

Year 4 Capstone：CHBE 453 — Biological Process and Product Design (8 cr)

Year 4 核心课：CHBE 481 — Bioprocess Engineering II (4 cr)

侧重生物工程：生物过程设计、生物制造、生物反应器。

📌 Note: Students entering 2018W+ must complete Term 1 of third year before entering Term 2. Technical electives and complementary studies electives should follow the UBC Academic Calendar requirements.
