Mechanical Engineering Program

Bachelor of Applied Science (BASc) · Department of Mechanical Engineering · 6 Program Options

📋 项目概览 Program Overview

机械工程专业在大二通过获奖的 Mech 2 项目建立核心基础，大三和大四学生在 6 个方向 (Options) 中选择一个进行深入学习。方向录取在大一末（提前录取）或大二末（标准录取），基于学业成绩和兴趣。

MECH features the award-winning Mech 2 program in Year 2, followed by 6 specialized Options in Years 3-4. Admission to Options at end of Year 1 (early) or Year 2 (standard), based on academic achievement and demonstrated interest.

📙 Mech 2 说明

Mech 2 是 UBC 获奖的综合大二项目。数学课程（MATH 253, MATH 256）嵌入在 MECH 221/222 中教授。MECH 220* 需在大二 Term 1 前修读。

The award-winning Mech 2 program integrates mathematics (MATH 253/256) within engineering science courses. MECH 220 taken before Term 1.
