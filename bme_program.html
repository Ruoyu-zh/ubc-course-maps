Biomedical Engineering Program

Bachelor of Applied Science (BASc) · School of Biomedical Engineering (SBME)

📋 项目概览 Program Overview

生物医学工程是 UBC 工程学院的独立本科专业，由生物医学工程学院（SBME）管理。该专业已获得加拿大工程认证委员会（CEAB）全面认证。课程涵盖生物力学、生物仪器、生物信息学、细胞工程和医疗设备设计，培养能够将工程原理应用于医学和生命科学的跨学科人才。

The Biomedical Engineering program is a standalone BASc degree administered by the School of Biomedical Engineering (SBME), fully accredited by the Canadian Engineering Accreditation Board. The curriculum spans biomechanics, bioinstrumentation, bioinformatics, cellular engineering, and medical device design.

🔑 核心课程简介 Core Course Highlights

BMEG 257 · Biomedical Engineering Design I (4 cr)
首个生物医学工程设计课程：从需求识别到原型开发，涵盖设计方法论、团队协作和医疗设备开发流程。
First design course: from need identification to prototyping — design methodology, teamwork, and medical device development process.
BMEG 321 · Biomedical Instrumentation (3 cr)
医疗仪器原理：传感器技术、生物信号采集、信号调理电路和医疗设备安全标准。
Medical instrument principles: sensor technology, biosignal acquisition, signal conditioning, and safety standards.
BMEG 350 · Human Structure/Function (4 cr)
从细胞到系统的人体结构与功能，为工程师提供必要的解剖学和生理学基础。
Human structure and function from cells to systems — essential anatomy and physiology foundation for engineers.
BMEG 457 · Capstone Design Project (6 cr)
生物医学工程毕业设计项目，与行业合作完成从概念到原型的完整工程设计。
Capstone design project with industry partners — complete engineering design from concept to prototype.

💡 与 ELEC Biomedical Option 的区别：BME 是独立的 BASc 学位（SBME 管理），课程更侧重于生物医学核心知识（生物学、化学、生物力学）。ELEC Biomedical Option 是电气工程学位下的一个方向（ECE 管理），保留更多 EE 核心课程。

Difference from ELEC Biomedical Option: BME is a standalone BASc degree (SBME-administered) with greater emphasis on biomedical core (biology, chemistry, biomechanics). The ELEC Biomedical Option is a concentration within the Electrical Engineering degree (ECE-administered), retaining more EE core courses.
